# National Registration Database (NRD)

## 中文解释 / English Explanation

* **定义 / Definition**  
  **中文解释**：国家注册数据库（NRD）是由加拿大证券管理局（CSA）创建和维护的一个电子系统，旨在集中管理证券行业从业人员的注册信息。它简化了注册流程，提高了监管效率，并确保信息的透明性和一致性。  
  **English Explanation**: The National Registration Database (NRD) is an electronic system created and maintained by the Canadian Securities Administrators (CSA) to centralize the registration information of securities industry professionals. It streamlines the registration process, enhances regulatory efficiency, and ensures transparency and consistency of information.

* **应用 / Application**  
  **中文解释**：NRD主要用于证券公司和投资顾问的注册、更新和注销。它还被用于监管机构对从业人员的合规性审查和投资者保护。使用NRD时，需注意数据的准确性和及时更新，以避免合规风险。  
  **English Explanation**: The NRD is primarily used for the registration, renewal, and termination of securities firms and investment advisors. It is also utilized by regulators for compliance reviews and investor protection. When using the NRD, it is crucial to ensure data accuracy and timely updates to avoid compliance risks.

* **重要性 / Significance**  
  **中文解释**：从监管角度看，NRD提高了证券行业的透明度和规范性；从投资角度看，它为投资者提供了更可靠的从业人员信息；从行业角度看，它简化了注册流程，降低了运营成本。  
  **English Explanation**: From a regulatory perspective, the NRD enhances transparency and standardization in the securities industry; from an investment perspective, it provides investors with more reliable information about professionals; from an industry perspective, it simplifies the registration process and reduces operational costs.

## 历史典故 / Historical Context

* **起源 / Origin**  
  **中文解释**：NRD于2003年由加拿大证券管理局推出，旨在取代传统的纸质注册系统。这一举措是为了应对证券行业日益复杂的监管需求和技术进步带来的挑战。  
  **English Explanation**: The NRD was launched in 2003 by the Canadian Securities Administrators to replace the traditional paper-based registration system. This initiative was aimed at addressing the increasingly complex regulatory demands of the securities industry and challenges posed by technological advancements.

* **影响 / Impact**  
  **中文解释**：NRD的推出显著提高了加拿大证券行业的监管效率和信息透明度。目前，它已成为加拿大证券从业人员注册的核心平台，并在全球范围内被视为电子化注册系统的典范。  
  **English Explanation**: The introduction of the NRD significantly improved regulatory efficiency and information transparency in Canada's securities industry. Today, it serves as the core platform for registering securities professionals in Canada and is regarded globally as a model for electronic registration systems.

## 要点总结 / Takeaway

* **中文**  
  - NRD是加拿大证券管理局创建的电子化注册系统。
  - 主要用于证券从业人员的注册、更新和合规审查。
  - NRD提高了行业透明度、简化了流程并降低了成本。

* **English**  
  - The NRD is an electronic registration system created by the Canadian Securities Administrators.
  - It is primarily used for registering, renewing, and conducting compliance reviews of securities professionals.
  - The NRD enhances industry transparency, simplifies processes, and reduces costs.
