# Qualified Longevity Annuity Contract (QLAC)

## 中文解释 / English Explanation

* **定义 / Definition**  
  **中文解释**：合格长寿年金合同（QLAC）是一种特殊的年金保险产品，允许个人将其退休账户中的一部分资金用于购买延迟支付的年金，以应对长寿风险。该产品由美国国税局（IRS）于2014年批准，旨在帮助退休人员确保在晚年有稳定的收入来源。核心概念包括延迟支付、税收优惠和长寿保障。  
  **English Explanation**: A Qualified Longevity Annuity Contract (QLAC) is a specialized annuity product that allows individuals to use a portion of their retirement account funds to purchase a deferred-payment annuity, addressing longevity risk. Approved by the IRS in 2014, it aims to help retirees secure a stable income source in their later years. Key concepts include deferred payments, tax advantages, and longevity protection.

* **应用 / Application**  
  **中文解释**：QLAC适用于担心退休后资金不足的投资者，尤其是那些预期寿命较长的人群。通过将部分退休储蓄转化为延迟支付年金，投资者可以在高龄时获得稳定收入。需要注意的是，QLAC的购买金额有上限（通常为退休账户余额的25%或$145,000），且支付起始年龄不得晚于85岁。  
  **English Explanation**: QLAC is suitable for investors concerned about outliving their retirement savings, particularly those with longer life expectancies. By converting part of their retirement savings into a deferred-payment annuity, investors can secure stable income in advanced age. Note that QLAC purchase amounts are capped (typically 25% of the retirement account balance or $145,000), and payments must begin by age 85.

* **重要性 / Significance**  
  **中文解释**：从监管角度看，QLAC为退休规划提供了税收优惠工具；从投资角度看，它帮助分散长寿风险；从行业角度看，它推动了年金产品的创新和市场发展。对于个人而言，QLAC是确保晚年财务安全的重要工具。  
  **English Explanation**: From a regulatory perspective, QLAC offers a tax-advantaged tool for retirement planning; from an investment perspective, it helps mitigate longevity risk; and from an industry perspective, it drives innovation and market growth in annuity products. For individuals, QLAC is a crucial tool for ensuring financial security in later life.

## 历史典故 / Historical Context

* **起源 / Origin**  
  **中文解释**：QLAC的概念源于对老龄化社会长寿风险的关注。2014年7月2日，美国国税局发布公告（Rev. Rul. 2012-26），正式批准了QLAC产品。这一决定基于对退休人员财务需求的深入研究和对年金市场潜力的评估。  
  **English Explanation**: The concept of QLAC originated from concerns about longevity risk in an aging society. On July 2, 2014, the IRS issued Notice (Rev. Rul. 2012-26), officially approving QLAC products. This decision was based on extensive research into retirees' financial needs and an assessment of the annuity market's potential.

* **影响 / Impact**  
  **中文解释**：QLAC的推出对保险行业产生了深远影响，促进了年金产品的多样化和创新。同时，它为全球其他国家和地区提供了应对老龄化问题的参考方案。目前，QLAC已成为美国退休规划中的重要组成部分。  
  **English Explanation**: The introduction of QLAC has had a profound impact on the insurance industry, driving diversification and innovation in annuity products. It also serves as a reference for other countries and regions addressing aging populations. Today, QLAC is an integral part of retirement planning in the U.S.
