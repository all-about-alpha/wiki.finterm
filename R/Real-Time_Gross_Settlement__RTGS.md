# Real-Time Gross Settlement (RTGS)

## 中文解释 / English Explanation

* **定义 / Definition**  
  **中文解释**：实时全额结算（RTGS）是一种支付系统，用于在交易发生时立即处理大额资金转账，且每笔交易单独结算，不进行净额轧差。该系统由中央银行或金融机构运营，确保资金转移的即时性和安全性。  
  **English Explanation**: Real-Time Gross Settlement (RTGS) is a payment system designed to process large-value fund transfers immediately as they occur, with each transaction settled individually without netting. Operated by central banks or financial institutions, it ensures the immediacy and security of fund transfers.

* **应用 / Application**  
  **中文解释**：RTGS广泛应用于银行间大额支付、证券交易结算、跨境支付等场景。由于其高实时性和低风险特性，它成为金融市场的核心基础设施。然而，系统的高成本和复杂性也带来了一定的运营风险。  
  **English Explanation**: RTGS is widely used in interbank large-value payments, securities settlement, and cross-border payments. Due to its high immediacy and low-risk nature, it serves as a core infrastructure in financial markets. However, the system's high cost and complexity also introduce certain operational risks.

* **重要性 / Significance**  
  **中文解释**：从监管角度看，RTGS有助于维护金融系统的稳定性；从投资角度看，它提高了资金流动效率；从行业角度看，它是现代金融体系不可或缺的一部分。  
  **English Explanation**: From a regulatory perspective, RTGS helps maintain the stability of the financial system; from an investment perspective, it enhances the efficiency of fund flows; from an industry perspective, it is an indispensable part of modern financial systems.

## 历史典故 / Historical Context

* **起源 / Origin**  
  **中文解释**：RTGS的概念最早于20世纪80年代提出，随着电子支付技术的发展而逐步实现。1985年，美国联邦储备系统率先推出Fedwire系统，成为全球首个RTGS系统。此后，各国央行纷纷效仿并建立自己的RTGS系统。  
  **English Explanation**: The concept of RTGS was first proposed in the 1980s and gradually implemented with advancements in electronic payment technology. In 1985, the U.S. Federal Reserve launched Fedwire, the world's first RTGS system. Subsequently, central banks worldwide followed suit and established their own RTGS systems.

* **影响 / Impact**  
  **中文解释**：RTGS的引入显著提升了全球金融市场的效率和安全性。它不仅减少了支付风险，还促进了跨境贸易和投资的发展。目前，几乎所有发达国家和部分发展中国家都已采用RTGS系统作为其金融基础设施的核心部分。  
  **English Explanation**: The introduction of RTGS significantly enhanced the efficiency and security of global financial markets. It not only reduced payment risks but also facilitated cross-border trade and investment. Today, almost all developed countries and some developing nations have adopted RTGS systems as a core part of their financial infrastructure.

## 要点总结 / Takeaway

* **中文**  
  - RTGS是处理大额资金转账的实时全额结算系统。  
  - 广泛应用于银行间支付、证券结算和跨境交易等领域。  
  - 作为现代金融体系的核心基础设施之一，其重要性不可忽视。

* **English**  
  - RTGS is a real-time gross settlement system for processing large-value fund transfers.  
  - Widely used in interbank payments, securities settlement, and cross-border transactions.  
  - As a core infrastructure of modern financial systems, its significance cannot be overlooked.
