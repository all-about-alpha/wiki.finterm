# Acquisition

## 中文解释 / English Explanation

* **定义 / Definition**  
  - **中文解释**  
    收购（Acquisition）是指一家公司通过购买另一家公司的全部或部分股权或资产，从而获得对该公司的控制权或所有权。收购可以是友好的（双方同意）或敌意的（未经目标公司管理层同意）。核心概念包括股权收购、资产收购、横向收购、纵向收购和混合收购。  
  - **English Explanation**  
    Acquisition refers to the process by which one company purchases all or a portion of another company's shares or assets to gain control or ownership. It can be friendly (with mutual agreement) or hostile (without the target company's management consent). Key concepts include equity acquisition, asset acquisition, horizontal acquisition, vertical acquisition, and conglomerate acquisition.

* **应用 / Application**  
  - **中文应用场景**  
    收购广泛应用于企业扩张、市场整合、技术获取和资源优化等场景。例如，互联网公司通过收购初创企业获取新技术，或传统企业通过收购竞争对手扩大市场份额。需要注意的是，收购可能面临整合风险、文化冲突和监管审查等问题。  
  - **English Application**  
    Acquisitions are widely used for business expansion, market consolidation, technology acquisition, and resource optimization. For instance, tech companies acquire startups to gain new technologies, or traditional firms acquire competitors to expand market share. Risks include integration challenges, cultural clashes, and regulatory scrutiny.

* **重要性 / Significance**  
  - **中文重要性**  
    从监管角度看，收购可能涉及反垄断审查；从投资角度看，收购是资本运作的重要手段，能提升企业估值；从行业角度看，收购推动产业整合和技术进步。例如，中国互联网行业的多次大规模收购重塑了市场格局。  
  - **English Significance**  
    From a regulatory perspective, acquisitions may trigger antitrust reviews. From an investment standpoint, they are a key tool for capital operations, enhancing corporate valuation. From an industry perspective, acquisitions drive consolidation and technological advancement. For example, major acquisitions in the global tech industry have reshaped market dynamics.

## 历史典故 / Historical Context

* **起源 / Origin**  
  - **中文起源**  
    收购的起源可以追溯到19世纪末的工业革命时期，当时企业通过横向收购实现规模经济。例如，美国标准石油公司通过收购竞争对手垄断了石油市场。20世纪80年代，敌意收购兴起，杠杆收购（LBO）成为热门工具。  
  - **English Origin**  
    The origin of acquisitions dates back to the late 19th century during the Industrial Revolution, when companies pursued horizontal acquisitions to achieve economies of scale. For example, Standard Oil monopolized the oil market by acquiring competitors. In the 1980s, hostile takeovers emerged, and leveraged buyouts (LBOs) became a popular tool.

* **影响 / Impact**  
  - **中文影响**  
    收购对行业格局产生了深远影响。例如，中国互联网巨头通过收购快速扩展业务，形成了“BAT”（百度、阿里巴巴、腾讯）三足鼎立的局面。当前，全球范围内的跨境收购日益增多，推动了经济全球化。  
  - **English Impact**  
    Acquisitions have profoundly impacted industry landscapes. For instance, Chinese tech giants rapidly expanded through acquisitions, creating the "BAT" (Baidu, Alibaba, Tencent) dominance. Today, cross-border acquisitions are increasingly common, driving economic globalization.

## 要点总结 / Takeaway

* **中文**  
  - 核心价值：收购是企业快速扩张和资源整合的重要手段。  
  - 使用场景：适用于技术获取、市场扩展和行业整合。  
  - 延伸意义：收购不仅是资本运作工具，也是战略布局的关键。  

* **English**  
  - Key Point 1: Acquisitions are a vital tool for rapid expansion and resource integration.  
  - Key Point 2: They are applicable for technology acquisition, market expansion, and industry consolidation.  
  - Key Point 3: Beyond capital operations, acquisitions are crucial for strategic positioning.
