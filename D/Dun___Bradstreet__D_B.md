# Dun & Bradstreet (D&B)

## 中文解释 / English Explanation

* **定义 / Definition**  
  **中文解释**  
  邓白氏（Dun & Bradstreet，简称D&B）是一家全球领先的商业数据和分析公司，成立于1841年，总部位于美国新泽西州。其核心业务是提供企业信用评级、商业信息、风险管理解决方案以及市场分析服务。D&B通过其庞大的全球数据库，帮助企业和金融机构评估合作伙伴的信用风险，优化供应链管理，并支持商业决策。  
  **English Explanation**  
  Dun & Bradstreet (D&B) is a globally renowned business data and analytics company, founded in 1841 and headquartered in New Jersey, USA. Its core business revolves around providing business credit ratings, commercial information, risk management solutions, and market analysis services. Leveraging its extensive global database, D&B assists businesses and financial institutions in assessing the credit risk of partners, optimizing supply chain management, and supporting commercial decision-making.

* **应用 / Application**  
  **中文应用场景**  
  D&B的服务广泛应用于企业信用评估、供应链管理、市场拓展和风险管理等领域。例如，企业在选择供应商时，可以通过D&B的信用报告评估其财务健康状况；金融机构在发放贷款前，也会参考D&B的信用评级来降低违约风险。需要注意的是，D&B的数据和分析结果虽然权威，但仍需结合其他信息进行综合判断。  
  **English Application**  
  D&B's services are widely used in business credit assessment, supply chain management, market expansion, and risk management. For instance, companies can evaluate the financial health of potential suppliers using D&B's credit reports, while financial institutions may refer to D&B's credit ratings to mitigate default risks before issuing loans. It is important to note that while D&B's data and analysis are authoritative, they should be used in conjunction with other information for comprehensive decision-making.

* **重要性 / Significance**  
  **中文重要性**  
  从监管、投资和行业角度来看，D&B在商业信用评估领域具有重要地位。其信用评级和数据分析为全球企业提供了透明、可靠的决策依据，有助于降低交易风险、提高市场效率。同时，D&B的服务也支持金融机构的合规管理，满足监管机构对风险控制的要求。  
  **English Significance**  
  From regulatory, investment, and industry perspectives, D&B holds a significant position in the field of business credit assessment. Its credit ratings and data analysis provide transparent and reliable decision-making tools for global businesses, helping to reduce transaction risks and improve market efficiency. Additionally, D&B's services support financial institutions in compliance management, meeting regulatory requirements for risk control.

## 历史典故 / Historical Context

* **起源 / Origin**  
  **中文起源**  
  邓白氏的历史可以追溯到1841年，由Lewis Tappan在纽约创立，最初名为“The Mercantile Agency”，旨在为商人提供信用信息。后来，Robert Graham Dun接管了公司，并将其更名为R.G. Dun & Company。1933年，R.G. Dun与John M. Bradstreet创立的Bradstreet Company合并，正式成立邓白氏（Dun & Bradstreet）。  
  **English Origin**  
  The history of Dun & Bradstreet dates back to 1841 when Lewis Tappan founded "The Mercantile Agency" in New York to provide credit information to merchants. Later, Robert Graham Dun took over the company and renamed it R.G. Dun & Company. In 1933, R.G. Dun merged with the Bradstreet Company, founded by John M. Bradstreet, officially forming Dun & Bradstreet (D&B).

* **影响 / Impact**  
  **中文影响**  
  邓白氏的成立和发展对全球商业信用评估行业产生了深远影响。其信用评级体系成为行业标准，广泛应用于国际贸易和金融领域。如今，D&B的数据库覆盖全球数亿家企业，为跨国公司和中小企业提供了重要的信用支持。  
  **English Impact**  
  The establishment and growth of Dun & Bradstreet have had a profound impact on the global business credit assessment industry. Its credit rating system has become an industry standard, widely used in international trade and finance. Today, D&B's database covers hundreds of millions of businesses worldwide, providing critical credit support to multinational corporations and small-to-medium enterprises alike.

## 要点总结 / Takeaway

* **中文**

  - 应用场景：企业信用评估、供应链管理、市场拓展、金融机构风控。  
  - 延伸意义：推动商业透明化，降低交易风险，提升市场效率。  

* **English**  
  - Core Value: Offers global business credit ratings and commercial data analysis to support risk management and decision-making.  
  - Usage Scenarios: Business credit assessment, supply chain management, market expansion, and financial institution risk control.  
  - Extended Meaning: Promotes business transparency, reduces transaction risks, and enhances market efficiency.
