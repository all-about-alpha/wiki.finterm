# Leveraged Lease

## 中文解释 / English Explanation

* **定义 / Definition**  
  **中文解释**：杠杆租赁是一种融资租赁形式，其中出租人通过借款（通常为设备或资产价值的60%-80%）来购买租赁资产，并将该资产出租给承租人。出租人通过租金收入和税收优惠获得回报，而承租人则获得资产的使用权。  
  **English Explanation**: A leveraged lease is a form of financing lease where the lessor borrows a significant portion (typically 60%-80% of the asset's value) to purchase the leased asset and leases it to the lessee. The lessor earns returns through rental income and tax benefits, while the lessee gains the right to use the asset.

* **应用 / Application**  
  **中文应用场景**：杠杆租赁常用于大型设备、飞机、船舶等高价值资产的融资。其优势在于降低出租人的资金压力，同时为承租人提供灵活的融资选择。但需注意利率风险和信用风险。  
  **English Application**: Leveraged leases are commonly used for financing high-value assets such as large equipment, aircraft, and ships. They reduce the lessor's capital burden while offering flexible financing options for the lessee. However, risks such as interest rate fluctuations and credit risks must be considered.

* **重要性 / Significance**  
  **中文重要性**：从监管角度看，杠杆租赁有助于优化资本结构；从投资角度看，它为投资者提供了稳定的现金流和税收优惠；从行业角度看，它推动了高价值资产的流通和使用。  
  **English Significance**: From a regulatory perspective, leveraged leases optimize capital structures; from an investment standpoint, they provide stable cash flows and tax benefits; from an industry perspective, they facilitate the circulation and utilization of high-value assets.

## 历史典故 / Historical Context

* **起源 / Origin**  
  **中文起源**：杠杆租赁起源于20世纪60年代的美国，最初用于飞机和铁路设备的融资。随着金融创新的发展，逐渐扩展到其他高价值资产领域。  
  **English Origin**: Leveraged leases originated in the United States in the 1960s, initially used for financing aircraft and railway equipment. With financial innovation, they expanded to other high-value asset sectors.

* **影响 / Impact**  
  **中文影响**：杠杆租赁在全球范围内推动了高价值资产的融资和流通，特别是在航空、航运和能源行业。目前，它已成为国际金融市场中的重要工具之一。  
  **English Impact**: Leveraged leases have facilitated the financing and circulation of high-value assets globally, particularly in aviation, shipping, and energy industries. Today, they are a vital tool in international financial markets.

## 要点总结 / Takeaway

* **中文**  
  1. 核心价值：通过借款降低资金压力，实现高价值资产的灵活融资。
  2. 使用场景：适用于飞机、船舶、大型设备等高价值资产的租赁。
  3. 延伸意义：优化资本结构，提供税收优惠和稳定现金流。

* **English**  
  1. Core Value: Reduces capital pressure through borrowing and enables flexible financing for high-value assets.
  2. Usage Scenarios: Suitable for leasing high-value assets like aircraft, ships, and large equipment.
  3. Extended Meaning: Optimizes capital structures while offering tax benefits and stable cash flows.
