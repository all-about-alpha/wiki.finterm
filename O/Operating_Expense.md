# Operating Expense

## 中文解释 / English Explanation

* **定义 / Definition**  
  **中文解释**：运营费用（Operating Expense，简称OPEX）是指企业在日常经营活动中产生的、与主营业务直接相关的费用。这些费用通常包括租金、工资、公用事业费、办公用品、维修维护等。运营费用不包括资本支出（如购买设备或建筑物的费用），而是与企业的日常运营紧密相关。  
  **English Explanation**: Operating Expense (OPEX) refers to the costs incurred by a business in its day-to-day operations that are directly related to its core business activities. These expenses typically include rent, salaries, utilities, office supplies, and maintenance. OPEX does not include capital expenditures (such as purchasing equipment or buildings) but is closely tied to the ongoing operations of the business.

* **应用 / Application**  
  **中文应用场景**：运营费用广泛应用于企业的财务报表中，特别是在损益表中。企业通过分析运营费用来控制成本、提高效率。例如，零售企业会关注租金和员工工资等运营费用，以确保其盈利能力。需要注意的是，过高的运营费用可能会侵蚀企业的利润，因此企业需要合理控制这些费用。  
  **English Application**: Operating expenses are widely used in financial statements, particularly in the income statement. Businesses analyze OPEX to control costs and improve efficiency. For example, retail companies focus on operating expenses such as rent and employee salaries to ensure profitability. It is important to note that excessively high operating expenses can erode profits, so businesses need to manage these costs effectively.

* **重要性 / Significance**  
  **中文重要性**：从监管、投资和行业角度来看，运营费用是衡量企业运营效率的重要指标。投资者通过分析企业的运营费用来判断其成本控制能力和盈利能力。监管机构也会关注企业的运营费用，以确保其财务健康。对于行业而言，运营费用的高低直接影响企业的竞争力和市场地位。  
  **English Significance**: From regulatory, investment, and industry perspectives, operating expenses are a key metric for assessing a company's operational efficiency. Investors analyze OPEX to evaluate a company's cost control and profitability. Regulatory bodies also monitor operating expenses to ensure financial health. For industries, the level of operating expenses directly impacts a company's competitiveness and market position.

## 历史典故 / Historical Context

* **起源 / Origin**  
  **中文起源**：运营费用的概念最早可以追溯到工业革命时期，当时企业开始大规模生产，需要系统地记录和管理日常运营成本。随着会计学的发展，运营费用逐渐成为财务报表中的重要组成部分。20世纪初，随着现代企业管理理论的兴起，运营费用的管理变得更加精细化。  
  **English Origin**: The concept of operating expenses dates back to the Industrial Revolution when businesses began mass production and needed to systematically record and manage day-to-day operational costs. With the development of accounting, OPEX became an essential part of financial statements. In the early 20th century, with the rise of modern management theories, the management of operating expenses became more refined.

* **影响 / Impact**  
  **中文影响**：运营费用的管理对企业的财务健康和竞争力有着深远的影响。随着全球化的发展，企业面临更加复杂的运营环境，运营费用的控制成为企业成功的关键因素之一。目前，许多企业通过技术手段（如自动化、数据分析）来优化运营费用，以提高效率和盈利能力。  
  **English Impact**: The management of operating expenses has a profound impact on a company's financial health and competitiveness. With globalization, businesses face more complex operational environments, making OPEX control a critical factor for success. Today, many companies use technological tools (such as automation and data analysis) to optimize operating expenses, thereby improving efficiency and profitability.

## 要点总结 / Takeaway

* **中文**  
  - **核心价值**：运营费用是衡量企业日常运营成本的重要指标，直接影响企业的盈利能力。  
  - **使用场景**：广泛应用于财务报表分析、成本控制和效率提升。  
  - **延伸意义**：运营费用的管理反映了企业的运营效率和战略执行能力。

* **English**  
  - **Key Point 1**: Operating expenses are a crucial metric for assessing a company's day-to-day operational costs and directly impact profitability.  
  - **Key Point 2**: Widely used in financial statement analysis, cost control, and efficiency improvement.  
  - **Key Point 3**: The management of operating expenses reflects a company's operational efficiency and strategic execution capabilities.
