# Over the Top (OTT)

## 中文解释 / English Explanation

* **定义 / Definition**  
  **中文解释**：OTT（Over the Top）是指通过互联网直接向用户提供内容或服务，绕过传统的有线电视、电信运营商等中间渠道。核心概念包括内容分发、流媒体技术和用户直接访问。  
  **English Explanation**: OTT (Over the Top) refers to the delivery of content or services directly to users over the internet, bypassing traditional intermediaries such as cable TV or telecom operators. Core concepts include content distribution, streaming technology, and direct user access.

* **应用 / Application**  
  **中文解释**：OTT广泛应用于视频流媒体（如爱奇艺、腾讯视频）、音乐流媒体（如网易云音乐）和即时通讯（如微信）。需要注意的是，OTT服务可能面临网络带宽限制和版权问题。  
  **English Explanation**: OTT is widely used in video streaming (e.g., Netflix, Hulu), music streaming (e.g., Spotify), and instant messaging (e.g., WhatsApp). It is important to note that OTT services may face challenges such as bandwidth limitations and copyright issues.

* **重要性 / Significance**  
  **中文解释**：从监管角度看，OTT推动了内容分发的创新；从投资角度看，OTT平台成为资本市场的热门标的；从行业角度看，OTT重塑了媒体和通信行业的格局。  
  **English Explanation**: From a regulatory perspective, OTT drives innovation in content distribution; from an investment perspective, OTT platforms are hot targets in capital markets; from an industry perspective, OTT reshapes the landscape of media and communication industries.

## 历史典故 / Historical Context

* **起源 / Origin**  
  **中文解释**：OTT的起源可以追溯到2000年代初，随着宽带互联网的普及和流媒体技术的成熟，Netflix等平台开始通过互联网提供视频内容，标志着OTT时代的开启。  
  **English Explanation**: The origin of OTT can be traced back to the early 2000s when broadband internet became widespread and streaming technology matured. Platforms like Netflix began delivering video content over the internet, marking the beginning of the OTT era.

* **影响 / Impact**  
  **中文解释**：OTT对传统电视和电信行业产生了深远影响，推动了“剪线族”现象的出现。目前，全球范围内OTT服务已成为主流内容消费方式之一。  
  **English Explanation**: OTT has had a profound impact on traditional TV and telecom industries, leading to the rise of "cord-cutting." Today, OTT services have become one of the mainstream ways of consuming content globally.

## 要点总结 / Takeaway

* **中文**  
  - 核心价值：通过互联网直接向用户提供内容和服务。
  - 使用场景：视频流媒体、音乐流媒体、即时通讯等。
  - 延伸意义：推动内容分发创新，重塑媒体和通信行业。

* **English**  
  - Key Point: Delivering content and services directly to users over the internet.
  - Usage Scenarios: Video streaming, music streaming, instant messaging.
  - Extended Meaning: Driving innovation in content distribution and reshaping media and communication industries.
