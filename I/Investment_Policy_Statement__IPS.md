# Investment Policy Statement (IPS)

## 中文解释 / English Explanation

* **定义 / Definition**  
  **中文解释**：投资政策声明（IPS）是一份正式文件，由投资顾问或资产管理公司为客户制定，明确投资目标、风险承受能力、资产配置策略以及投资限制等核心内容。它通常由金融专业人士根据客户的具体需求和市场环境编制，旨在为投资决策提供指导框架。  
  **English Explanation**: An Investment Policy Statement (IPS) is a formal document created by an investment advisor or asset management firm for a client, outlining investment objectives, risk tolerance, asset allocation strategies, and investment constraints. It is typically developed by financial professionals based on the client’s specific needs and market conditions, serving as a guiding framework for investment decisions.

* **应用 / Application**  
  **中文解释**：IPS广泛应用于个人投资者、机构投资者（如养老基金、捐赠基金）以及家族办公室等场景。它为投资者提供了明确的投资方向和风险管理标准，帮助避免情绪化决策。需要注意的是，IPS应根据市场变化和客户需求定期更新，以确保其有效性。  
  **English Explanation**: IPS is widely used in scenarios involving individual investors, institutional investors (such as pension funds and endowments), and family offices. It provides clear investment direction and risk management standards, helping to avoid emotional decision-making. It is important to note that IPS should be regularly updated based on market changes and client needs to ensure its effectiveness.

* **重要性 / Significance**  
  **中文解释**：从监管角度看，IPS是合规管理的重要工具，确保投资活动符合法律法规和客户利益。从投资角度看，它为资产配置和风险管理提供了科学依据。从行业角度看，IPS提升了专业服务的透明度和信任度，促进了资产管理行业的规范化发展。  
  **English Explanation**: From a regulatory perspective, IPS is a crucial tool for compliance management, ensuring that investment activities align with legal requirements and client interests. From an investment perspective, it provides a scientific basis for asset allocation and risk management. From an industry perspective, IPS enhances the transparency and trustworthiness of professional services, promoting the standardized development of the asset management industry.

## 历史典故 / Historical Context

* **起源 / Origin**  
  **中文解释**：IPS的概念起源于20世纪70年代的美国，当时养老基金和机构投资者开始寻求更系统化的投资管理方法。1974年《雇员退休收入保障法》（ERISA）的颁布进一步推动了IPS的普及，要求受托人制定明确的投资政策以保护受益人利益。此后，IPS逐渐成为全球资产管理行业的标准实践。  
  **English Explanation**: The concept of IPS originated in the United States in the 1970s when pension funds and institutional investors began seeking more systematic approaches to investment management. The enactment of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act (ERISA) in 1974 further popularized IPS by requiring trustees to establish clear investment policies to protect beneficiaries' interests. Since then, IPS has become a standard practice in the global asset management industry.

* **影响 / Impact**  
  **中文解释**：IPS的引入显著提升了资产管理行业的专业性和规范性。它不仅帮助投资者实现长期财务目标，还降低了因缺乏规划而导致的投资风险。如今，IPS已成为全球范围内个人和机构投资者的重要工具，广泛应用于财富管理和退休规划等领域。  
  **English Explanation**: The introduction of IPS has significantly enhanced the professionalism and standardization of the asset management industry. It not only helps investors achieve long-term financial goals but also reduces investment risks caused by lack of planning. Today, IPS has become an essential tool for individual and institutional investors worldwide, widely applied in wealth management and retirement planning.

## 要点总结 / Takeaway

* **中文**  
  - **核心价值**：为投资决策提供科学框架和明确方向。  
  - **使用场景**：个人理财、机构资产管理、家族办公室等。  
  - **延伸意义**：提升透明度、规范行业实践、保护投资者利益。

* **English**  
  - **Key Point 1**: Provides a scientific framework and clear direction for investment decisions.  
  - **Key Point 2**: Used in personal finance, institutional asset management, family offices, etc.  
  - **Key Point 3**: Enhances transparency, standardizes industry practices, and protects investor interests.
