# Electronic Federal Tax Payment System (EFTPS)

## 中文解释 / English Explanation

* **定义 / Definition**  
  **中文解释**：电子联邦税务支付系统（EFTPS）是由美国财政部开发和管理的一种电子支付系统，旨在帮助个人和企业通过互联网或电话安全地支付联邦税款。该系统允许用户安排和跟踪税款支付，确保支付的准确性和及时性。  
  **English Explanation**: The Electronic Federal Tax Payment System (EFTPS) is an electronic payment system developed and managed by the U.S. Department of the Treasury, designed to help individuals and businesses securely pay federal taxes online or by phone. The system allows users to schedule and track tax payments, ensuring accuracy and timeliness.

* **应用 / Application**  
  **中文解释**：EFTPS广泛应用于美国纳税人，特别是企业和自雇人士，用于支付所得税、工资税和其他联邦税款。用户可以通过该系统提前安排支付日期，避免逾期罚款。需要注意的是，使用EFTPS需要提前注册，并确保银行账户信息准确无误。  
  **English Explanation**: EFTPS is widely used by U.S. taxpayers, particularly businesses and self-employed individuals, to pay income taxes, payroll taxes, and other federal taxes. Users can schedule payment dates in advance to avoid late penalties. It is important to note that registration is required to use EFTPS, and accurate bank account information must be provided.

* **重要性 / Significance**  
  **中文解释**：EFTPS在税务管理中的重要性体现在其高效性和安全性。它为纳税人提供了便捷的支付方式，减少了纸质支票的使用，降低了支付错误的风险。从监管角度看，EFTPS有助于提高税收征管的透明度和效率，确保税款及时入库。  
  **English Explanation**: The significance of EFTPS in tax administration lies in its efficiency and security. It provides taxpayers with a convenient payment method, reduces the use of paper checks, and minimizes the risk of payment errors. From a regulatory perspective, EFTPS enhances the transparency and efficiency of tax collection, ensuring timely revenue collection.

## 历史典故 / Historical Context

* **起源 / Origin**  
  **中文解释**：EFTPS于1996年由美国财政部推出，旨在响应电子支付技术的快速发展，并减少传统纸质支付方式的低效性。该系统最初仅面向企业用户，后来逐步向个人纳税人开放。其开发背景是20世纪90年代美国政府推动的“电子政务”计划。  
  **English Explanation**: EFTPS was launched in 1996 by the U.S. Department of the Treasury in response to the rapid development of electronic payment technology and to address the inefficiencies of traditional paper-based payment methods. Initially designed for businesses, it was later expanded to individual taxpayers. Its development was part of the U.S. government's "e-government" initiative in the 1990s.

* **影响 / Impact**  
  **中文解释**：EFTPS的推出显著改变了美国税务支付的方式，推动了电子支付的普及。它不仅提高了支付效率，还减少了税务部门的运营成本。目前，EFTPS已成为美国联邦税务支付的主要渠道之一，广泛应用于全球范围内的美国纳税人。  
  **English Explanation**: The introduction of EFTPS significantly transformed the way federal taxes are paid in the U.S., promoting the adoption of electronic payments. It not only improved payment efficiency but also reduced operational costs for tax authorities. Today, EFTPS is one of the primary channels for federal tax payments and is widely used by U.S. taxpayers globally.

## 要点总结 / Takeaway

* **中文**  
  - **核心价值**：EFTPS提供了一种安全、便捷的联邦税务支付方式，确保税款支付的准确性和及时性。  
  - **使用场景**：适用于个人和企业支付所得税、工资税等联邦税款。  
  - **延伸意义**：EFTPS的普及推动了电子支付在税务领域的应用，提升了税收征管的效率。

* **English**  
  - **Key Point 1**: EFTPS offers a secure and convenient method for paying federal taxes, ensuring accuracy and timeliness.  
  - **Key Point 2**: It is used by individuals and businesses to pay income taxes, payroll taxes, and other federal taxes.  
  - **Key Point 3**: The widespread adoption of EFTPS has promoted the use of electronic payments in tax administration, enhancing efficiency.
